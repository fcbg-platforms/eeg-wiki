Vamp
====

BrainProduct ``V-amp`` amplifiers are available on the CSRU and EEG platform. The
``V-amp`` is a small amplifier powered via USB and capable of recording up to 16
channels connected via touchproof connectors and 2 auxiliary channels.

.. note::

    The ``V-amp`` amplifiers will power on as soon as they are connected to the USB
    port, they do not have an ``ON/OFF`` button.
